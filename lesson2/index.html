Design Principles

White Space: Amazon

  • This is a depiction of the white space and clean design principle. Amazon has white space and padding around all of their products. It segments the individual products properly and has a nice look to it.

    • Hick's Law Green Magic Homes

  • This shows Hick's law pretty well. It only gives one option - Learn more. This minimizes the amount of time people have to think about what they want. If they are on their website, they probably want to learn more and that option is provided. Seen better on a larger display.

    • Proximity Wix

  • This website shows proximity. Because the items are grouped together in an organized way, and they are close to one another, we consider them all part of one group. If they were spread out evenly, we wouldn't know where one stopped and the next started. Seen better on a larger display.
