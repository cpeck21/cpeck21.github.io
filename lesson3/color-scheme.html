Color Scheme

Dominant Color: Royal Blue

This color will be used in my title, headers, and footers. It also will be used to highlight any important information in the website. Blue is known for distilling confidence in people. This will help people have confidence in the information provided in my website.

Complimentary Colors: Tan and Royal Blue

My navigation bar will be Navy blue to offset the royal blue. If anything else needs to be highlighted or set apart, I will use a tan color.
